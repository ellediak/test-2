Let Me Tell You About Myself

Hi, My name is Elle Diakovasilis and I am 22 years old. I am currently studying at Australian Catholic University in Strathfield and am in my 4th year of study. University has allowed me to develop my skills and knowledge in all areas but most importantly, developed my social skills. I have had the opportunity to develop friendships with individuals who are pursuing the same career, which serves as a loving support mechanism for future endeavours.

Let me tell you more...

I am an absolute FOODIE! I love all things food, cooking, creating recipes and sharing the food I make on my food page! Cooking isn't just a hobby for me; it's a passion that drives my creativity in the kitchen. I find joy in experimenting with ingredients, developing new recipes, and providing delicious meals for my family. Through my food page, I share my love of cooking with others by providing step-by-step cooking demonstrations and inspiring meal ideas. Growing up in a Greek family, my passion for food goes beyond just enjoying a good meal; it's about preserving traditions, sharing flavours, and bringing family together. Family is at the heart of everything I do, and I cherish the moments spent cooking and sharing meals with loved ones. Alongside my love for food, I have a strong passion for teaching. Whether I'm guiding someone through a recipe on my food page or sharing a meal idea, I find immense joy in helping others learn and discover their own love for food. As I began to ponder on possible careers, I combined my love for food, teaching, and being in the kitchen to discover that becoming a food technology teacher would be the perfect opportunity for me. It would provide me with passion, and joy to do something I love, rather than something I have to do. It fills my day with purpose as I get to share what I love with students whom I care about. I aim to inspire future generations and enable them to develop the same love for food that I have.
